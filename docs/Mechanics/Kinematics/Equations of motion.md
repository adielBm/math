## Motion in One Dimension

>  consts: $a$ = constant acceleration, $v_{0}$ = inital velocity, $x_{0}$ = inital position

### Zero Acceleration

- $v=v_{0}$
- $x=x_{0}+v_{0}t$

### Constant Acceleration

- $v=v_{0}+at$
- $x=x_{0}+v_{0}t+{\frac{1}{2}}at^2$
- $v^2=v_{0}^2+2a(x-x_{0})$
- $(x-x_{0})=\left( \frac{v_{0}+v}{2} \right)t$
